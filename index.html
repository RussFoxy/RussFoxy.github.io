Welcome to the Kellogg Volleyball Club Website





The Kellogg Volleyball Club is organized and run by business school students at the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University, Evanston, IL. The club is open to all Kellogg students and alumni, both full and part time, as well as JVs. Aside from weekly practices on campus, the club participates in:

  • Intramurals
  • MBA Tournaments with other schools
  • Beach volleyball days
  • Social events
